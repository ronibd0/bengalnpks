South Bengal Fertilizer Mills Ltd. is the pioneer of mixed fertilizer in Bangladesh. Mixed Fertilizer of SBFML is well known as Bengal NPKS with two grades one for vegetables known as Bengal NPKS Rabi and one for Paddy.

South Bengal Fertilizer Mills Ltd. Pioneer of mixed fertilizer in Bangladesh. Bengal NPKS a product pride for farmers in Bnagladesh

South Bengal Fertilizer mills Ltd, is one the main affiliated concern of Noapara group established as a limited (Pvt.) company in 2013. It produces various grades of NPKS fertilizers approved by Government as per market demand in its state of art modern factory. The plant also has a packaging unit specialized to pack various sulphates.
